## Technical Philosophy

### Maximum Efficiency
- **No Bloatware**: Every dependency must justify its existence
- **Lean Stack**: PostgreSQL + Redis + Node.js + Prisma
- **Docker-First**: Reproducible environments, zero config drift

### High Trust System
- **Trust Score Algorithm**: Multi-dimensional user reputation
  - Review quality
  - Verification status
  - Community engagement
  - Historical accuracy
  
- **Local Guide Badge System**: Gamified expertise recognition
  - Bronze: 10+ quality reviews
  - Silver: 50+ reviews + 80% helpful rating
  - Gold: 200+ reviews + 90% helpful rating + verified identity
  - Platinum: 500+ reviews + community moderator status

### Dynamic Business Attributes
- Flexible schema for diverse business types
- JSON-based attribute storage
- Real-time updates
- Category-specific fields
